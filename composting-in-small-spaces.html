Composting in Small Spaces

No garden? No problem!

Learn some of the methods of composting food scraps using wormeries or the 'bokashi method'. No garden required. If you grow plants on your windowsills, patio or balcony, this workshop is for you.

A friendly, hands-on workshop where you can find out all about:

  • The bokashi method - compost ALL your leftovers, pest and odour-free
  • Composting directly into plant pots and containers
  • How to choose and set up a wormery
  • Troubleshooting, help and advice

Thanks to Birmingham Food Legends Fund, we will have bokashi buckets and bran for you to take home and help you get composting.
