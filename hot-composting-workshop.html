Hot Composting Workshop

Learn how to make compost in just 6-8 weeks!

Hot composting is the fastest way to turn your garden waste and food scraps into rich, crumbly compost. This hands-on workshop will teach you everything you need to know to get started.

In this workshop you'll learn:

  • The science behind hot composting
  • How to build and maintain a hot compost heap
  • The perfect mix of green and brown materials
  • How to troubleshoot common problems
  • Ways to use your finished compost

All participants will receive a detailed guide to take home, plus the opportunity to purchase discounted compost bins.
