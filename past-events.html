Celebrating Our Partnerships

Over the past three years, we've been privileged to work with an inspiring range of community projects and organizations across Birmingham. With generous support from the National Lottery Community Fund and Birmingham Food Legends, we've been able to bring composting solutions to neighborhoods throughout the city.

We're proud to have worked with these fantastic partners:

  • #IncredibleSurplus
  • The Active Wellbeing Society
  • St Paul's Community Trust
  • Friends of the Earth Birmingham
  • Martineau Gardens (urban wildlife haven)
  • Moor Pool Hall Trust (heritage community space)
  • EcoBirmingham (environmental action group)
  • Masefield Community Gardens (local food growing)
  • Stanhope Community Centre
  • Wheelers Lane Allotment Committee
  • Highfield Hall Community Centre
  • Birmingham and Black Country Wildlife Trust

Each collaboration has brought new learning opportunities and helped us demonstrate how composting can benefit communities in different ways - from reducing food waste to creating rich soil for urban gardens. We're grateful to all our partners for their enthusiasm and commitment to making Birmingham greener, one compost heap at a time!
